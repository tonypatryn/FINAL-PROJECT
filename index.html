What is Exquisite Corpse?

Exquisite corpse, also known as exquisite cadaver (from the original French term cadavre exquis) or rotating corpse, is a method by which a collection of words or images is collectively assembled. Sort of a long form Mad-Libs. Each collaborator adds to a composition in sequence, either by following a rule (e.g. "The adjective noun adverb verb the adjective noun", as in "The green duck sweetly sang the dreadful dirge") or by being allowed to see only the end of what the previous person contributed.

How does it work?

You and your friends pick a title for your story. Think of something fun, yet descriptive.

Pick how many friends will play along. The more people there are, the better it is!

Start writing your story, one person at a time! Remember, you can only see the last three words the person in front of you wrote!
