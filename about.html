About Tony

An artist since he could hold a pencil, Tony has been drawing for over 27 years. He created Patrynize.com when he was but a wee sixteen year old in high school, designing posters for plays and other school events including redesigning the school newspaper. He then attended Columbia College of Chicago for a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Graphic Design in 2007.

Tony is also a six-time winner of the Young Authors Contest in Illinois and a published poet. He is currently working on his first novel, Negative Tuesday, that he hopes to release in 2017.
